A JOURNEY TO THE MEDIEVAL PAST

William Marshal was one of the greatest knight who ever lived.

Starting life with modest means, William rose through the ranks of Normans to become a wealthy landed knight and the 1st Earl of Pembroke. He served four Plantagenet Kings (Henry II, Henry the Younger, Richard the Lionheart & King John) and one powerful queen ( Eleanor of Aquitaine). Loyal to a fault, he was well respected among his fellow barons for his diplomacy, strength of will, and prowess in war.
